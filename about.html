Namaste and many greetings from Nepal.
      

Nepal Hiking designs tailor-made active travel experiences in wild and wonderful destinations in a manner that is organized with lots of adventures and fun. Safety of our clients is an important responsibility for our team so the trips are organized with full safety without losing its sense of freedom. Our trips allow you to explore the beauty of nature eliminating the annoyance of daily life across Nepal, Tibet and Bhutan. We are happy to say that we, Adventure Treks Nepal is a reputed adventure travel, tour and trekking operator, registered and licensed by the government of Nepal. We specialize in offering Trekking, Peak Climbing, Himalayan expeditions, biking tours, white water river rafting, Jungle safari, domestic Flight Tickets and Hotel Booking etc. Our trips are designed for everyone, for clients of all ages and levels of experience. We are local Tour and Trek operators and we personally operate each trip to ensure a reasonable cost and best value. Your safety, comfort and satisfaction are of utmost priority. We are dedicated to make your holiday with us, a memorable one.
